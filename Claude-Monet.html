Claude Monet's notable work

Impression, Sunrise

This is a painting by Claude Monet first shown at what would become known as the "Exibition of the Impressionists" in Paris. This painting is credited with inspiring the name of the Impressionist movement.

Rouen Cathedral series

The paintings in the series each capture the facade of the Rouen Cathedral at different times of the day and year and reflect changes in its appearance under different lighting conditions.

London Parliament series

Claude Monet painted a series of oil paintings of the Palace of Westminster, home of the British Parliament during stays in London. All of the series' paintings share the same viewpoint from Monet's window at St Thomas' Hospital overlooking the Thames.

Water Lilies

Is a series of approximately 250 oil paintings by French Impressionist Claude Monet. The paintings depict his flower garden at his home in Giverny, and were the main focus of his artistic production during the last thirty years of his life.
